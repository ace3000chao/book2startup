# Scaling Up — 关键术语词典 (阶段1产出)

> 术语提取器产出 | 包含author_definition + key_distinction | 不做筛选

---

- id: g01
  term: Gazelles（瞪羚企业）
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第1章 Overview
  author_definition: |
    "Gazelles are high-impact firms, relatively old (average 25 years), rare (2-3% of all firms), but they account for almost all of the private sector employment and revenue growth in the economy."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "快速倒闭的Startup" — Gazelles不是短命快速公司，而是持续增长25年+的长寿公司
    ≠ "增长快的任何公司" — Gazelles有具体的规模门槛（$10M-$1B+）
    = 高增长、高影响、长寿的Scaleup企业
  why_it_matters: |
    Scaling Up整本书的核心受众就是Gazelles企业家。区分"增长快但短命"
    和"持续Scale up的长寿企业"是整本书的前提。
  tags: [core-concept, target-audience, scaling]

- id: g02
  term: BHAG (Big Hairy Audacious Goal)
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第1章 Overview
  author_definition: |
    "A Big Hairy Audacious Goal to achieve in the next 10 to 25 years. Like summiting Everest, it's a constant reference point that guides all decisions."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "Vision/愿景" — BHAG是25年级方向，Vision通常5-10年
    ≠ "年度目标" — BHAG不是可以当年完成的指标
    ≠ "Mission/使命" — BHAG是地标，使命是存在的理由
    = 25年不移的地标，战略的北极星
  why_it_matters: |
    BHAG是7 Strata of Strategy第7层的内容，也是OPSP的核心元素。
    没有BHAG，战略就没有长期方向锚点。
  tags: [core-concept, vision, long-term]

- id: g03
  term: FACe (Function Accountability Chart)
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第3章 The Leaders
  author_definition: |
    "A one-page tool listing all the functions that must be filled in the organization, who's accountable for each, and their KPIs and outcomes."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "组织架构图" — FACe是'谁对什么结果负责'，不是层级汇报关系
    ≠ "工作描述" — FACe强调透明的问责和KPIs，不是职位职责列表
    = 功能问责表，强调结果而非活动
  why_it_matters: |
    FACe是Scaling Up People的核心工具，确保"对的屁股在对的椅子上"。
    后续的PACe、Manager as Coach等概念都以此为基础。
  tags: [people, accountability, organization]

- id: g04
  term: PACe (Process Accountability Chart)
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第3章 The Leaders
  author_definition: |
    "A one-page tool listing the 4-9 key processes that drive the business horizontally, who's accountable for each process, and 2-3 KPIs tracking process health."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "流程手册/SOP" — PACe是横向问责表，不是详细的步骤文档
    ≠ "FACe" — FACe是纵向（职能），PACe是横向（流程）
    = 横向流程问责，打破职能竖井
  why_it_matters: |
    复杂度增加时，流程横跨多个职能，往往没人负责。
    PACe为每个核心流程指定唯一负责人，防止责任真空。
  tags: [process, accountability, cross-functional]

- id: g05
  term: 4 Decisions
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第1章 Overview
  author_definition: |
    "The four key decision areas every business must get right to scale: People, Strategy, Execution, and Cash."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "部门划分" — 4 Decisions不是告诉你哪些部门存在，而是说要搞定哪些领域的决策
    ≠ "管理重点" — 4 Decisions是'做对'，不只是'关注'
    = 企业Scaling必须搞定的四个决策维度
  why_it_matters: |
    整本书的结构就是按4 Decisions组织的。
    任何一项有短板，企业就无法有效Scale。
  tags: [core-framework, decision-making]

- id: g06
  term: Rockefeller Habits
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第1章 Overview
  author_definition: |
    "10 fundamental habits that support successful strategy execution, derived from John D. Rockefeller's management practices 100 years ago."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "通用管理习惯" — Rockefeller Habits是经过100年验证的具体习惯清单
    ≠ "任何管理最佳实践" — 只有在Rockefeller体系内验证过的才算
    = 执行力的习惯基础，不可或缺的运营节奏
  why_it_matters: |
    Rockefeller Habits是Scaling Up Execution的核心框架。
    10个习惯从高管团队健康→战略对齐→数据驱动→文化激活，覆盖执行全貌。
  tags: [execution, habits, checklist]

- id: g07
  term: Topgrading
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第4章 The Team
  author_definition: |
    "A hiring methodology co-created by Brad and Geoff Smart that helps leaders hire A Players more than 90% of the time, using Job Scorecards and serial behavioral interviews."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "结构化面试" — Topgrading包含Job Scorecard、序列行为面试、深度背景调查
    ≠ "行为面试法" — Topgrading远超一般行为面试，包含3-4小时深度回顾
    = 系统化的A类人才招聘方法论
  why_it_matters: |
    糟糕招聘的代价是15倍年薪。
    Topgrading是Scaling Up People的关键工具，使命"招聘到对的人"的核心方法。
  tags: [hiring, methodology, a-players]

- id: g08
  term: A Player
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第4章 The Team
  author_definition: |
    "Someone in the top 10% of the available talent pool who is willing to accept your specific offer."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "最好的人才" — A Player是相对的可获得人才池，不是绝对的最优秀
    ≠ "最贵的人才" — A Player可能薪资合理，只要在目标人才池的前10%
    = 可获得且愿意加入的前10%人才
  why_it_matters: |
    A类人才是Scaling People的前提。
    一个A类人才等于三个B/C类人才的生产力，是所有Scaling的基础。
  tags: [hilling, talent, definition]

- id: g09
  term: X-Factor
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第7章 7 Strata
  author_definition: |
    "A 10x to 100x underlying advantage over the competition that completely wipes out any and all rivals."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "竞争优势" — X-Factor必须是10-100倍的差距，不只是"好一点"
    ≠ "差异化" — 差异化可以是5-10%，X-Factor必须能彻底碾压对手
    = 压倒性的底层优势
  why_it_matters: |
    X-Factor是7 Strata of Strategy的第6层，是战略的核心护城河。
    如果X-Factor轻易被复制，说明战略设计失败。
  tags: [strategy, competitive-advantage]

- id: g10
  term: Core Values
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第6章 The Core
  author_definition: |
    "A handful of rules (usually 3-7) that define acceptable behavior and guide all relationship decisions in the company."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "墙上的标语" — Core Values只有被HR系统执行才有意义
    ≠ "公司愿景/使命" — Core Values是规则，使命是存在的理由
    ≠ "企业文化描述" — Core Values是具体的、可以判断对错的行为规则
    = 指导所有人事决策的行为规则清单
  why_it_matters: |
    Core Values是Scaling Up People的基础。
    没有Core Values，招聘/绩效/奖励/晋升都没有依据，文化会随Scale稀释。
  tags: [culture, values, people]

- id: g11
  term: OPSP (One-Page Strategic Plan)
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第8章
  author_definition: |
    "A single-page planning tool with seven columns (Who, What, When, Where, How, Why, Shouldn't) designed to drive alignment, accountability, and focus."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "传统商业计划书" — OPSP只有一页，不是几十页的计划书
    ≠ "战略文档摘要" — OPSP包含执行部分（后四列），不只是战略
    = 战略+执行的一页工具
  why_it_matters: |
    OPSP是Scaling Up Strategy的核心工具，
    也是"让全员在同一页上"的关键沟通工具。
  tags: [strategy, planning, alignment]

- id: g12
  term: Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第12章 The Cash
  author_definition: |
    "How long it takes, after you spend a dollar on rent, utilities, payroll, inventory, marketing, etc., for it to make its way through your business model and back into your pocket."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "利润周期" — CCC衡量的是现金，不是会计利润
    ≠ "应收账款周转" — CCC是完整一圈，涵盖采购到收款全流程
    = 现金从花出到收回的完整周期
  why_it_matters: |
    增长吃现金是创业引力第一定律。
    CCC越短，企业现金流越健康，Scale能力越强。
  tags: [cash, financial-metrics]

- id: g13
  term: Growth Paradox
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第2章 Barriers
  author_definition: |
    "The belief that as you scale the company, things should get easier — but they actually get harder and more complicated."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "成长的正常困难" — 成长悖论是系统性的，不只是"变大变难"
    ≠ "管理风格问题" — 成长悖论是复杂度的数学必然，不是管理者的失败
    = 规模增长导致复杂度指数上升的必然现象
  why_it_matters: |
    认识成长悖论是克服它的第一步。
    书中所有工具（Rockefeller Habits、FACe、PACe等）都是用来破解这个悖论的。
  tags: [growth, complexity, paradox]

- id: g14
  term: Routine Sets You Free
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第1章 Overview
  author_definition: |
    "Goals without routines are wishes; routines without goals are aimless. Establishing correct habits (routines) is what enables true freedom, not just having goals."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "越努力越自由" — 这里强调的是正确习惯的杠杆效应
    ≠ "例行公事" — Routine是有意识的、战略性的习惯体系，不是机械重复
    = 有目标支撑的习惯才是真正的自由之路
  why_it_matters: |
    Routine Sets You Free是Scaling Up方法论的底层哲学。
    整本书的10大Rockefeller Habits就是这句话的操作化表达。
  tags: [habits, philosophy, execution]

- id: g15
  term: 7 Strata of Strategy
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第7章
  author_definition: |
    "Seven components of a robust strategy: 1. Words You Own, 2. Sandbox and Brand Promises, 3. Brand Promise Guarantee, 4. One-PHRASE Strategy, 5. Differentiating Activities, 6. X-Factor, 7. Profit per X and BHAG."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "一般战略框架" — 7 Strata是针对Growth Firm设计的，不是通用战略模型
    ≠ "战略SWOT分析" — 7 Strata关注差异化护城河，不只是内部分析
    = 针对中层市场的战略构建框架
  why_it_matters: |
    7 Strata是Scaling Up Strategy的核心工具。
    比McKinsey 7-S更轻，比简单战略更全面，专为Growth Firm设计。
  tags: [strategy, framework]

- id: g16
  term: The Council（战略议会）
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第7章
  author_definition: |
    "A separate strategic thinking team of 3-5 people meeting weekly to focus exclusively on strategic issues, distinct from the operational executive team meeting."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "高管例会" — 战略议会是独立于日常运营会议的战略性讨论
    ≠ "董事会" — 议会是高管内部的战略小组，不是外部治理机构
    = 高管内部的战略思考小组
  why_it_matters: |
    战略议会确保战略思考不被日常运营淹没。
    书中最核心的建议之一："战略不能每年只做一次"。
  tags: [strategy, governance, meeting]

- id: g17
  term: Brand Promises
  type: term
  source_chapter: 第7章 7 Strata
  author_definition: |
    "Three specific promises you make to your core customers (e.g., Southwest Airlines: Low Fares, Lots of Flights, Lots of Fun), with Kept Promise Indicators to track delivery."
  key_distinction: |
    ≠ "品牌定位" — Brand Promises是具体的、可衡量的客户承诺
    ≠ "价值主张" — Brand Promises有3个且必须可追踪
    = 客户可明确感知的具体承诺
  why_it_matters: |
    Brand Promises是7 Strata的第2层，是战略的核心落地。
    每一个Promises都必须有对应的追踪指标（KPI）。
  tags: [brand, strategy, customer]
